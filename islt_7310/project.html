Projects

Goal 1: Apply knowledge of foundational principles in library and information science.

Description: I am gaining knowledge about the foundations of library and information science. I have completed assignments in required and elective courses to demonstrate the ability to apply the knowledge and values of this discipline to specific areas and problems.

Reflection: I am learning to gather, analyze, interpret, find and prepare information professionally. I am learning to collaborate in group projects. I am gaining computer and technology skills to create, present and complete information projects in Zoom, Canvas, PowerPoint, MSWord, screen-casting software, and LibGuides.

Artifacts:

 1. Lesson plan PowerPoint  2. Thesaurus 

Goal 2: Gain skills to creatively adapt to future changes.

Description: I am learning to apply professional, technical and soft skills to information environments. I have learned methods of managing, collaborating, assessing and evaluating in libraries and information agencies.

Reflection: I am gaining knowledge about collections, reference services, library funding, technology, and more. I am improving my technological and soft skills to increase my ability to help patrons. In retrospect, I need to volunteer in an information environment more often to strengthen these skills.

Artifacts:

 1. Grant narrative  2. Advocacy letter 

Goal 3: Develop technology skills to apply in various ways to information settings.

Description: I am learning about the vast array of technological products, software, skills, and processes that improve the information environment in which we are immersed. I am applying these skills and learning news ways to use technology in libraries.

Reflection: Technology is integrated into libraries and information agencies. I am learning that technology is mostly an asset, but that you should always have a plan B. Coming from a nontechnological background, I am learning to allow for extra time to complete assignments that require new technology. I am learning how to use various software products, social networking, HTML5, CSS3 and the internet effectively.

Artifacts:

 1. IT paper  2. Library guides tutorial
