Reflection

Personal Reflection Statement

I believe in the power of libraries. Libraries connect people, teach literacy, strengthen communities, and promote lifelong learning. As I seek to obtain a Master of Library & Information Science degree, I am gaining valuable knowledge about foundational principles and theories to apply to current practices within this discipline. I am learning valuable technology skills to help libraries adapt and contribute in a global digital environment. My goal is to apply the skills and knowledge I am learning to contribute to libraries and information science agencies.

Professional competencies and skills

Determined, dedicated graduate student gaining valuable knowledge and skills to become a future information professional. Learning to apply foundational theories and principles about the history and current practice of library and information science. Service-oriented, eager to learn, dedicated to helping libraries promote information literacy, strengthen community/student engagement, and develop a passion for lifelong learning. Key assignments completed include building tutorials, developing and delivering information literacy instruction, collaborating to create a collection development policy, creating thesaurus entries, grant-writing, and more.
